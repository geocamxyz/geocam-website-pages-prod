Common Questions

How accurate are the clearance measurements?

GeoCam delivers 10 cm accuracy with centimeter-precision measurements. This is suitable for vegetation management planning and documentation. Measurements reflect actual field conditions at the time of capture.

Can we capture from the ground or do we need bucket trucks?

GeoCam uses street-level 360 imagery captured from vehicles. No bucket trucks, climbing, or specialized equipment required. Drive your circuits and capture everything visible from the roadway.

How do we share data with tree crews?

Export work locations as GeoJSON, Shapefile, or KML for their GPS devices. Share imagery viewer links so crews can see exact conditions before arriving on site. No software installation required for viewing.

Can we track before/after conditions?

Yes. Capture corridors before trim work, then recapture after completion. GeoCam maintains historical imagery so you can document clearances achieved and verify contractor work.

Does it work for transmission ROW?

Yes. GeoCam works for both distribution circuits and transmission right-of-way. Capture conditions from access roads or ROW paths accessible by vehicle.

How fast can we capture a circuit?

Typical capture speed is 15-25 mph depending on conditions. Most crews capture 50-100+ miles of corridor per day. Processing is automated after upload.
