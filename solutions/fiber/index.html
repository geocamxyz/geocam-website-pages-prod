FAQ

Common Questions

What pole data do I get?

GPS coordinates, attachment identification, height measurements, clearance data, and violation flagging. Schema is customizable to your GIS requirements.

How does this compare to traditional pole surveys?

GeoCam captures imagery once; you extract data from your desk. One small crew can cover what used to require multiple field teams. Fewer truck rolls, faster turnaround.

Is accuracy sufficient for make-ready engineering?

10cm accuracy from visual positioning. Suitable for preliminary inventory, route planning, and make-ready screening. We recommend validating against control points for your specific conditions.

Can contractors access the imagery?

Yes. Generate secure view links for specific routes. Useful for construction coordination, QA verification, and reducing site visits.

What about data center interconnects?

Same platform supports long-haul and metro route studies, low-latency path design, and constructability assessments for DCI projects.

How do I start?

Start with a 1-week sprint at $1,000—includes camera kit, 150 miles of processing, and one data model. Scale from there.
