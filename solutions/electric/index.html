FAQ

Common Questions

What distribution data do I get?

Pole inventory with GPS, height, class, and material. Equipment tags for transformers, switches, reclosers, capacitors. Attachment heights and counts. Conductor span documentation. All exportable to your GIS schema.

How do you handle indoor substations?

Walk capture mode works indoors where conventional capture cannot reach. Capture equipment, control cabinets, cable trays, and nameplates. Generate 3D visualizations for training and planning.

Does this work with ArcGIS Utility Network?

Yes. GeoCam data can be migrated into Esri Utility Network solutions. We support the Electric Utility Network Foundation schema and can map to your custom configurations.

What about nameplate data?

High-resolution 360° capture lets you read nameplate data from imagery. Extract manufacturer, ratings, and serial numbers without sending crews back to the field.

How accurate are the measurements?

10cm accuracy from visual positioning. Suitable for pole heights, attachment clearances, vegetation distances, and equipment locations. Not a replacement for licensed survey where required.

How do we get started?

Start with a 1-week sprint at $1,000. Pilot a distribution circuit and a substation to validate the workflow for your specific asset types and GIS requirements.
