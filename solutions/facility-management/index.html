FAQ

Common Questions

What can we capture indoors?

Hallways, rooms, mechanical spaces, equipment rooms, common areas—any accessible interior space. Walk mode captures continuous 360° imagery that links to your floor plans.

How does this integrate with our CMMS?

Export asset data to standard formats (GeoJSON, CSV) for import into Cityworks, Maximo, or other CMMS/IWMS platforms. API access available for deeper integrations.

Can we measure from the imagery?

Yes. 10cm accuracy from visual positioning lets you measure room dimensions, equipment clearances, and distances directly in the viewer without returning to the building.

How do we handle multiple buildings?

Each building becomes a separate dataset in your account. Navigate between buildings from a single dashboard. Scale capture across your entire portfolio over time.

What about secure or restricted areas?

Role-based access controls let you restrict visibility to specific buildings, floors, or areas. Share only what each user needs to see.

How do we get started?

Start with a 1-week sprint at $1,000—includes camera kit, SaaS viewer, and one data model. Pilot one building to validate the workflow before scaling.
