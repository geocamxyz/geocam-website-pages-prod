CAD Modeling

CAD data we build—LOD 100 to 500.

GeoCam capture can support CAD deliverables, formatted to flow into the software you already use (AutoCAD, Revit, Rhino, SolidWorks, and more). Because every model starts from measured field conditions, we're especially strong at as-built and high-LOD deliverables (LOD 300–500)—design coordination, scan-to-BIM, and facility handoff.

LOD 100Conceptual

Massing & approximate context.

LOD 200Approximate

Generic elements, approximate size & location.

LOD 300Precise

Accurate geometry for design & documentation.

LOD 350Coordinated

Element connections for clash detection.

LOD 400Fabrication

Detail for fabrication & assembly.

LOD 500As-built

Field-verified record conditions.
