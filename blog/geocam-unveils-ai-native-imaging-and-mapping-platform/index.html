GEOCAM UNVEILS AI-NATIVE IMAGING AND MAPPING PLATFORM

The first AI Native Reality Capture Platform - capture imagery on vehicles, drones, walking and indoors. A single system to fuse data together and automate map data creation.

*AI-Native Reality Capture Platform Enables 3D Mapping and Monitoring for all kinds of Outdoor and Indoor Infrastructure.*

EL SEGUNDO, CA - July 11, 2024 – GeoCam, on a mission to simplify the way the world documents, analyzes and manages our shared infrastructure, today announced a new subscription service which enables users around the world to operate the GeoCam platform on their own. The GeoCam Reality Capture Platform is an AI-Native, 3D mapping solution that operates across a variety of environments (indoors and outdoors) and modalities (person mounted and vehicle mounted) was that was designed to be simple, scalable and flexible for any user.

“Until today the options for creating street level and indoor mapping data and imagery were limited by consumer grade tech that’s unable to produce quality data in complex environments or expensive traditional LiDAR based mobile mapping services. Today we are excited to announce that customers of all types can subscribe to the GeoCam system to use our cameras and software on their own. It is a pivotal moment for the GeoCam team,” said Myles Sutherland, CEO, GeoCam. “The photogrammetric-based GeoCam approach and technology simplifies the entire process, which has allowed us to lower the costs significantly and enable frequent data and imagery re-collects, supporting the monitoring or change detection that our customers need. Using GeoCam your team can view imagery over time, share and collaborate with each other, and most importantly work from accurate, current data produced by our imagery first data approach.”

What is unique about the GeoCam Platform

· Small, Simple Hardware + Operations - No LiDAR, No static capture requirements. Turn the camera on and start walking or/driving. You can move the camera around while you collect to capture all those tricky, hard to see parts of your indoor or outdoor infrastructure.

· Working in the Imagery. - No more heavy Point Clouds or 3D Mesh's to create 3D data. Users can work directly in GeoCam Imagery and quickly create accurate 3D GIS and CAD/BIM data.

· Management Built-in -. GeoCam SaaS takes care of the complexities in multiple field captures, data management, processing workflows, data sharing and integrations. Its an enterprise ready AI-Native Reality Capture System.

· Easy Data Integration - GeoCam data integrates directly with your existing business systems, including ArcGIS (online + enterprise) and many more.

“The tremendous flexibility and deep functionality of the GeoCam AI-native reality capture platform, which works seamlessly indoors and outdoors, is unrivaled,” said Mike Colosimo, COO, GeoCam. “Whether you need imagery to view existing conditions or need to create and maintain GIS (or CAD) data, GeoCam is the ideal platform for whatever your needs may be."
